About the Blog

CareerBridge Germany is a resource for anyone interested in Germany’s dual vocational training system, known as "Ausbildung". Learn how to apply, prepare for interviews, and move to Germany with confidence.

What is Ausbildung?

Ausbildung is a paid vocational training program in Germany that combines work experience with classroom learning. You earn while you learn and get a certified qualification recognized across the country.

  • Duration: 2 to 3.5 years
  • Fields: healthcare, IT, engineering, hospitality, and more
  • Salary: ~€800–€1,200/month depending on the year and field

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak German?
Yes, most programs require at least B1 level of German.

Do I need a visa?
Yes. A vocational training visa is available if you meet the requirements.

Is it open to non-EU citizens?
Absolutely! Many trainees come from Africa, Asia, and Latin America.
